Gazpacho

Description

Gazpacho is a cold soup that originates from the Andalusian region of Spain. It is made with fresh vegetables such as tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and onions, and seasoned with olive oil, vinegar, and garlic. Gazpacho is perfect for a refreshing summer meal, and can be served as a starter or main course.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 large cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, onion, and garlic. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  2. With the blender or food processor running, slowly pour in the olive oil and vinegar.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, and pulse to combine.
  4. Add the water and pulse until the soup reaches your desired consistency.
  5. Refrigerate the gazpacho for at least an hour before serving.
