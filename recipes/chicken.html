Chicken Parmesan

Description

A classic Italian-American dish made with breaded chicken breasts topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Place the chicken breasts between two sheets of plastic wrap and pound to an even thickness.
  3. In a shallow dish, combine the flour, garlic powder, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. In another shallow dish, beat the eggs.
  5. In a third shallow dish, place the breadcrumbs.
  6. Coat each chicken breast in the flour mixture, shaking off any excess.
  7. Dip each chicken breast in the beaten eggs, letting any excess drip off.
  8. Coat each chicken breast in the breadcrumbs, pressing the breadcrumbs onto the chicken to help them adhere.
  9. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  10. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 3-4 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  11. Transfer the chicken breasts to a baking dish.
  12. Spoon the tomato sauce over the chicken breasts.
  13. Sprinkle the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the tomato sauce.
  14. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  15. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.
